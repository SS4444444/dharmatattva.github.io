About Dharma Tatva

Dharma Tatva is a knowledge movement — not bound by borders, beliefs, or ideologies.

Born from a single idea — that dharma means duty — we exist to question, explore, and reimagine the world through sharp inquiry and unfiltered truth. At Dharma Tatva, we dive deep into the forces that shape our lives: from economics and power structures to culture, science, spirituality, and history. We don’t sell narratives. We unpack them.

Our mission is to build an informed community that understands its role in the larger fabric of society — not as passive consumers, but as conscious participants. Through video series, discussions, and public collaborations, we spark dialogue, inspire responsibility, and ignite change.

We are awareness-centric.
We are not right or left. We are depth-driven.

This isn’t just content — it’s a continuum. An exploration of what lies between God & Man, behind global power games, inside civilizations, and beyond the boundaries of science.

This is our bit of Dharma.
Do your bit.
